Bio

Hello! I'm Teresa LaBolle, a software developer who's happiest working on frontend design and crafting interfaces that feel both intuitive and delightful. I still care deeply about writing thoughtful, well-tested software, and I've worked on everything from automated testing and backend services to hardware-adjacent projects. However, I especially enjoy web development and UI work, where code and visual design intersect.

Outside of tech, you'll most likely find me hiking, crocheting, painting, diving into video games, or tackling a home-improvement project. Before moving into software, I also worked as a Mandarin medical interpreter and florist; these experiences taught me how to balance creativity with precision, listen carefully, and communicate clearly with the people I'm working with.
